SafeTrade is an automated low-frequency trading bot available to YFDAI holders as well as the general public.


SafeTrade is aimed at cryptocurrency traders seeking an algorithmic bot that keeps risk low and trades strategically on a decidedly lower frequency based on market technicals.. The SafeTrade bot is currently available to trade two pairings, BTC/USD and ETH/USD.




The YFDAI protocol allows users to stake YF-DAI, or actively participate in transaction validation.


Staking takes place on our proof-of-stake blockchain, through a dedicated staking decentralized application. With just a 72hr initial lock period users maintain control over their tokens. Users enjoy the benefit of having a fixed interest return rate of 72% annually (0.2% per day / 6% per month).




Farming enables YF-DAI token holders to pool liquidity in support of token trading.


Farming takes place on the Uniswap decentralized exchange which subsequently yields guaranteed returns in the form of Uniswap fees. The more liquidity a token holder deposits into a liquidity pool, the higher the participant's percentage of the total rewards. YFDAI offers 4 farming pools for liquidity providers, including 72 hour and 30,60, and 90 day locked liquidity pools.
