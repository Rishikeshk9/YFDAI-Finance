What is DeFi





YFDAI is focused on creating the most secure, transparent and user-friendly decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem in the wide and quickly growing DeFi industry.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that the users of our services are our primary driver for success.

Finance for the future

YFDAI is dedicated to providing quality services with the highest level of security while leading by example. That is why we have had the YFDAI protocol and our smart contracts audited and approved by one of the leading independent auditors in the blockchain industry, Blockchain Consilium. YFDAI are one of the very few DeFi projects that store tokens in fully audited, time locked smart contracts to eliminate all possible smart contract exploits.

Moreover, the YFDAI core team has locked team, advisor and marketing tokens in time-released smart contracts until December 2021, making the corresponding addresses public for the community to verify through monthly transparency reports.

YFDAI is constantly working to ensure that all our products and partners adhere to the highest possible standards of quality and security, passing rigorous due diligence checks in the process. Requirements for a project to list on the YFDAI LaunchPad include 100% token supply lock in advance, a reputable third party smart contract audit, locked liquidity for exchange listing, and no prior fundraising to date. YFDAI SafeSwap, our decentralized exchange, has similar stringent requirements for exchange listing.

In addition, we have a robust governance system where we actively encourage community participation in voting on key YFDAI decisions that will help direct the future development and growth of our DeFi ecosystem. This governance model is unique and allows for cost-free voting publicly verifiable by YFDAI token holders.
